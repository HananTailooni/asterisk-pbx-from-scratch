 ** Why IP Authentication? **

1- No SIP REGISTER process.
2- Simpler authentication model.
3- Common in enterprise environments.
4- Similar to how many telecom providers provision SIP trunks.

** Why separate dialplan contexts? **
This provides a security boundary between external callers and internal resources.
External callers should never have unrestricted access to internal extensions, feature codes, voicemail access numbers, or administrative dialplan entries. 
All inbound calls are first processed by the from-telnyx context, where routing decisions are explicitly controlled.

** Why descriptive endpoint names?** 
Object names improve readability and maintainability.

** Why direct_media=no? ** 
By setting:
direct_media=no
all RTP media flows through Asterisk.
Phone
   │
Asterisk
   │
Carrier

instead of
Phone ──────> Carrier
